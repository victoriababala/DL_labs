Fake News Detection Using Deep Learning

Field: Media Literacy & Education

In an era dominated by digital information, the ability to identify false or misleading news is critical. This project leverages deep learning models to detect fake news with high accuracy, empowering individuals and organizations to combat disinformation effectively.
